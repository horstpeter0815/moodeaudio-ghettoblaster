# HiFiBerry Hardware Research

## HiFiBerry Products
HiFiBerry produces high-quality audio hardware for Raspberry Pi.

## Main Product Lines

### DAC Products
- **DAC+**: Basic DAC
- **DAC+ ADC**: DAC with analog input
- **DAC2 HD**: High-resolution DAC
- **DAC+ DSP**: DAC with DSP processing

### Amplifier Products
- **AMP+**: Audio amplifier
- **AMP2**: More powerful amplifier

### Other Products
- **Digi+**: Digital audio output
- **Beocreate**: Audio system for Bang & Olufsen speakers
